MasterKart

MasterKart is a local multiplayer karting game. Drive karts on maps based on real world with your friends on one computer. Enjoy driving model based on real-life experience on keyboard or with a controller. Try improving your lap times sector by sector in time trial or defeat your opponents in races head to head.

The game was made as project in two game development courses on Masaryk university. I worked on it with Zdeněk Doležal and Jakub Sekanina and we want to continue the developement and hopefully release the game on Steam at some point (mainly to see how releasing game on steam works).

MasterKart is developed in Unity and currently consists of one indoor track, one outdoor "amateur" track and one outdoor "professional" track. I worked on the driving model, sound system, ingame UI, some 3D assets (kart model and some track assets) and more.

Link not aviable yet. Contact me if you want to try the lastest build!

SkiResortTycoon

Game prototype made in few weeks as an assignment in Game design course on Masaryk university. I worked on it with Zdeněk Doležal and we made it in Unity. It is mix of Zdeněk's idea to make snow resort tycoon builder game and my idea to make it look like maps in winter resorts where the main point is to show the ski lift, slopes and important buildings in simple visual style.

There are 3 types of ski lifts and 3 types of buildings in the game. Skiers spawn at the parking lot, then need to buy ticket and then can ski until they are tired. There is also financial screen where the player can manage loans and see how the ski resort is doing financially.

This was really interesting project and we will maybe continue with the developement in the future.

WebGL build!

Alcounter

My android app for keeping track of blood alcohol content while drinking, and not after like with other similar apps. Partly made as assignment in ITU course on BUT FIT with Zdeněk Doležal, and then finished in my free time. Made mainly to learn app making and publishing on Google Play Store.

This was my first bigger and complete android app. It is written in Java. It went through few iterations and I learned a lot by developing it.

VagonApp

My android app for recognizing info about railway wagons from their numerical code. Made with Petr Šopf as an assignment in PV239 - Vývoj aplikací pro mobilní platformy course. The app uses text recognizion library MLkit to recognize text from photo. The text is then parsed and error checked and info about the wagon is displayed.

This is my second bigger and complete android app. It is written in kotlin and I tried and learned lots of new things and technologies. I plan to make it recognize more things about the wagons in the future.

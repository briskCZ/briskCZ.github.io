SkiResortTycoon - ski resort management game

Used Technologies: Unity, Gimp

My responsibilites: Design, UI, building of ski lifts, slopes and buildings

About

Context

SkiResortTycoon was created as a university assignment by me and Zdeněk Doležal. The goal was to design and make a simple game to learn about game design. We made the prototype in a few weeks during the exam period and the final result is playable below.

The Idea

The idea of making a tycoon game about ski resorts arose because we are fans of both. We wanted the game to be visually similar to the interactive maps of ski resorts where players would be presented with an untouched mountain and would have to build ski lifts, slopes, infrastructure and buildings to transform it into a prospering ski resort.

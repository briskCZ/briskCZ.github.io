About

Context

PigeonPummel was created as a gamejam entry for the Ludum Dare 56 by me and Zdeněk Doležal. We wanted to try out what making a game for a gamejam is like and we came up with this.

The Idea

The theme for Ludum Dare 56 was "Tiny Creatures" and we had multiple ideas for what our game could be. After a while we decided that it will be a pigeon flying in a city trying to "hit" targets.
